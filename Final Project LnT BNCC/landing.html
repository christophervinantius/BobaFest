What is BobaFest ?

Answer? Click here.

You will not want to miss it, so stay tuned!

What kind of events can we take part in BobaFest ?

Feel excited already? Click here.

Here are the main events you can attend to in this event.

  • Establish Your Passion in Technology
  • Starting Your Own Technology Startup
  • Artificial Intelligence Development
  • Most Recent Technologies 2021
  • Success in a Big Technology Company

For further details for each events you can go straight to the event page. :D

What can we do in the BobaFest Technology Expo?

More details? Click here.

Technology Expo is a big fair that features a lot of recent technologies presentations and also technology workshops presented by technology companies participating in this event. You can visit a lot of companies in their booths to take a look on their technology products and also to have some brief discussion with the companies. Because the event is held online, the Expo will use online meeting system with breakout rooms for each booths that you can visit freely as you want. Hopefully you can get some helpful insights about technology after attending the Expo. :D
